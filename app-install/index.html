Step-by-step install guide
Pick your phone type below — the process takes about 30 seconds.
1

Open the link in Safari or Chrome

Works in both Safari and Chrome on iPhone. Open your browser and go to the link below.

Link: netifybuilds.com/app
2

Tap the Share button

Look for the Share button — it's usually at the bottom of the screen in Safari, or in the top right in Chrome. It should look something like this:

It should look something like this
3

Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the share sheet until you see "Add to Home Screen" and tap it.

4

Tap "Add" in the top right

A preview will appear. Tap Add in the top right corner. That's it — the app is now on your home screen.

1

Open Chrome on your Android

Works best in Chrome. Open your browser and go to the link below.

Link: netifybuilds.com/app
2

Tap the three dots menu

In the top right corner of Chrome, tap the three vertical dots to open the menu.

3

Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"

You'll see either "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" — tap it. Some Android phones show a banner at the bottom of the screen instead.

4

Tap "Install" or "Add"

Confirm the install and the app will appear on your home screen immediately.
